More about me!

Hey!

I'm Parniya Saeedzadeh. I am a 25-year-old girl from Tehran,Iran and currently I'm living in Milan, Italy. Back in time when I was just a high school girl, or maybe even earlier at elementry school, I wanted to be a computer Engineer, I did't know much about computers though.
Time passed and I was getting ready for the big entrance exam when I was 17 years old. I studied so hard to achieve what I have always dreamed about. Guess what? It happened. I got accepted to the public university in IT engineering which was each and every teenage person dream back then. When I got the result I figured out that I was between Top 1% among more than 181,000 Mathematic and Physics participants in the national entrance exam. Time has passed and my prespective and interests has changed and I found my deep down passion in my life, but one lesson that I got from that exam which was my first big exam in my life, was "Impossible is impossible for me" and I get what I really want and try for.

When I entered university, my passion and interests about design started to show up in my life. Actually to be honest, I have to say that this passion has been always with me since when I was a lil kid, I just did't understand it till when I got a bit more mature. I can remember that when I was a lil kid, I have always been making and editting photos of my friends, family etc with photoshop. I can also remember that once when I was 10 years old, I made my own birthday invitation card with photoshop. I always wanted to be unique and creative in whole my life :))

So after I started my bachelor in IT engineering, I started to go to some design classes beside my studies, like some creativity workshops, photoshop class and so on. then I applied to a company for a Graphic Design role and I got accepted. I started as an intern there and after a month I started my job at Pelakino Department beside my university. So this was my frist move in design world.
Soon I realized that there is a field in design in which is related somehow to Web and Applications which was my study field. I started to read about it first and BOooom, I found the love of my life :) So I got an internship in UI/UX design and I had finished it before I moved to Italy.

Meanwhile when I was at the last year of my bachelor, I decided to apply abroad and experience new things and adventures, also continue my studies. So I get to know Politecnico di Milano which has a really high ranking and I applied for that and the first try for me seemed to work so I got accepted in Master of science in Telecommunication Engineering. You may ask why engineering again? well, I would say, I never hated engineering and it has been always a benefit for me in my life and I have alwayd had this mission in my life to make a bridge between design and technology, and to be at both environments helped me alot.

You may also understood from my profile that I am a music geek, You can also checkout the "Music Life" section here. so here is a triangle of my life: Design, Tech and music. and I'm sure it is gonna be the same for ever since all of them are pretty important to me in my life.

So, now here I am, with quite good experience in design, I can say that I am standing on the rightest track of my life and I am enjoying each and every single moment of my life. To be honest, It is not just about jobs and experiences, design is inside me, I see it everywhere and I always want to see all the things in a different way than others, for example, I am always trying to do all the tasks in my life in the easiest way possible. Before start doing something, I just think about how I can do it in the easiest and fastest but best way possible. For me, there is "always" a better way which comes from a critical way of thinking :) I alway like to solve everyone's problem by bringing up the solutions in a creative way. This, makes me a really good problem solver. I never see any possible solution impossible and I have always tried all possible solutions to find the best one. for me "Impossible is impossible" as mentioned before. So all these things in me made me love my job, to give people the best experience possible I can.
Finally to help people solve their challenges, I will be the happiest person on earth :)
